🔒 How we protect your data
📱Your statement never leaves your phone. FinSentinel runs entirely inside your browser tab using JavaScript. There is no server receiving your files.
📡Zero network requests. The counter in the header above shows every outgoing request FinSentinel makes. It will always read 0. You can open browser DevTools → Network and verify this yourself.
✈️Works in airplane mode. Turn off WiFi right now and upload a statement. It still works — because your data never goes anywhere.
💾History stays on your device. Your past reports are saved in your browser's localStorage — the same place websites save your preferences. Only you can access it.
👁️Open source. Every line of analysis code is public on GitHub. Anyone can verify what FinSentinel does and doesn't do.
